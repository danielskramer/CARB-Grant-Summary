What the Grant Funds

This is a readiness grant focused on building a strong foundation for future clean transportation projects.

Planning & Assessment

Conduct needs assessments, feasibility studies, and develop master plans that integrate clean mobility with housing, land use, and public health.

Community Engagement

Fund crucial outreach, education, and organizing efforts to build community support and gather input on clean transportation options.

Capacity Building

Develop early-stage collaborations, establish strong partnerships, and create workforce training programs for clean transportation careers.

A Crucial Note on Operational Costs

While the grant's primary focus is planning, there has been **optimism from grant administrators that operational costs** (like driver labor or short-term pilot service expenses) **could be eligible**. We encourage applicants to clearly articulate how any short-term operational funding is essential to the planning and readiness objective (e.g., funding a temporary service pilot to inform a future long-term plan).
